Dungeons & Dragons Club

Step into a world of imagination at The Library at Crescent! Our all-ages Dungeons & Dragons Club welcomes adventurers of every experience level—whether you’re a seasoned hero or brand-new to the game. Join us for epic quests, creative storytelling, and a fun, supportive group where anyone can roll the dice and shape the adventure. Come try it out—you might just discover your new favorite way to explore worlds beyond our own!

Book Club

Discover great stories and even better conversations at The Library at Crescent’s Book Club! Open to all ages and reading levels, our club is a relaxed, friendly space where readers come together to share thoughts, explore new perspectives, and enjoy the simple joy of discussing a good book. Whether you’re a lifelong reader or just getting back into the habit, you’re invited to join us each month as we dive into new titles and connect through the stories we love.

Kid's Arts & Crafts Club

Bring your creativity to life at The Library at Crescent’s Arts & Crafts Club! Designed especially for kids, this fun, hands-on club lets young artists explore colors, textures, and imagination through a variety of simple projects. From painting and paper crafts to seasonal decorations, there’s always something new to make and take home. It’s a playful, welcoming space where kids can learn, create, and express themselves—one craft at a time!

Community Service Club

Join us at The Library at Crescent’s Community Service Club, where helping others brings our neighborhood closer together. Open to all ages, this club gives volunteers the chance to make a real difference through simple, impactful projects—like donation drives, park cleanups, and kindness initiatives. Whether you’re looking to give back, earn service hours, or meet others who care about the community, this is the perfect place to start. Together, we can build a stronger, kinder Crescent.
